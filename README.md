## 📌 Overview

The **Enterprise Healthcare Performance Dashboard** is a comprehensive Power BI solution designed to help healthcare organizations monitor **Safety, Quality, and Operational Efficiency** using industry-recognized **Leapfrog Hospital Safety Standards**.

The dashboard transforms complex clinical and operational datasets into intuitive executive dashboards, enabling hospital leadership to monitor key performance indicators, improve patient safety, identify operational risks, and support data-driven decision making.

This project demonstrates enterprise-level Business Intelligence practices including data modeling, KPI design, DAX calculations, interactive reporting, and executive dashboard development.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

Healthcare organizations require a centralized reporting platform to monitor critical quality and safety metrics.

This solution enables organizations to:

- Monitor Leapfrog quality measures
- Improve patient safety initiatives
- Measure operational efficiency
- Track hospital performance over time
- Reduce manual reporting effort
- Support executive decision making
- Analyze trends and compliance
- Deliver a single source of truth

Provides an overall view of hospital performance across multiple quality domains.

## Section 2
### Computerized Physician Order Entry (CPOE)

Measures physician order compliance and adoption.

**Key Metrics**

- CPOE Count
- Compliance Percentage
- Performance Gauge
- Historical Trends

---

## Section 2C
### Bar Code Medication Administration (BCMA)

Monitors medication administration safety.

**Key Metrics**

- Compliance Count
- Compliance Percentage
- Patient Scan %
- Medication Scan %
- Decision Support
- Override Reasons
- Department Performance

---

## Section 4
### Maternity Care

Monitors maternal care quality indicators.

**Key Metrics**

- Maternity Volume
- Cesarean Birth
- Episiotomy
- Bilirubin Screening
- DVT Prophylaxis
- Antenatal Steroids

## Section 9
### Outpatient Procedures

Provides operational and quality insights for outpatient services.

**Key Metrics**
